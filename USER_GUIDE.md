# Circlenomic Portal — User Guide

## Welcome to Circlenomic

Circlenomic is your gateway to a Network Society — a community where everyone contributes, everyone benefits, and no one is left behind.

This guide explains how to use every feature of your Circlenomic node.

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Install the App

Download Circlenomic from:
- **iOS**: App Store (search "Circlenomic")
- **Android**: Google Play Store
- **Desktop**: circlenomic.org/download
- **Web**: portal.circlenomic.org

### Step 2: Create Your Account

```
1. Open the app
2. Tap "Create Account"
3. Choose a username (this is your public identity)
4. Set a password
5. Save your backup phrase (shown once — write it down!)
6. Your personal node starts syncing
```

**What's happening**: Your device is becoming a node in the Circlenomic network. It creates a secure, encrypted identity that only you control.

### Step 3: Join a Community

```
1. Tap "Join Community"
2. Search for your neighborhood/city
3. Request membership
4. Wait for 1-2 existing members to vouch for you
5. Welcome! You're now part of the network
```

**Why vouching?**: Communities are built on trust. Existing members help ensure new members are real people committed to mutual aid.

### Step 4: Complete Digital Literacy

```
1. Tap "Learn" → "Get Started"
2. Complete the 4-week Digital Literacy course
3. Earn 20 time credits upon completion
4. Learn how to use all Circlenomic features
```

**This is required** to ensure everyone can participate fully. It's free, self-paced, and taught by the AI assistant.

## Time Banking

Time banking is the heart of Circlenomic. Every hour of work is valued equally.

### How It Works

```
1 hour of service = 1 time credit

Examples:
• 2 hours fixing a faucet = 2 time credits
• 1 hour tutoring math = 1.5 time credits (teaching bonus)
• 3 hours cooking for community event = 3 time credits
• 1 hour mentoring a new member = 1.25 time credits
```

### Offering a Service

```
1. Go to Time Bank → "Offer Service"
2. Fill in:
   - What you're offering (e.g., "Bicycle repair")
   - Category (e.g., "Mechanical")
   - Your availability (e.g., "Weekends, 10am-4pm")
   - Credits per hour (default: 1)
   - Description of what you do
3. Submit → Your service appears in the marketplace
4. People can book you directly
```

### Receiving a Service

```
1. Go to Time Bank → "Find Service"
2. Browse or search for what you need
3. Select a provider
4. Book a time
5. After the work is done:
   - Provider logs the hours
   - You confirm the work
   - Credits are transferred
```

### Logging Work

```
1. Go to Time Bank → "Log Work"
2. Select the service you provided
3. Enter hours worked
4. Add description (e.g., "Fixed leaky faucet, replaced washer")
5. Add photo proof (optional)
6. Submit → Receiver gets notification to confirm
```

## Marketplace

```
2. Browse by category:

4. Filter by:
   - Distance (local优先)
```

### Listing an Item

```
1. Go to Marketplace → "Sell Something"
2. Choose type: Good, Service, or Skill
3. Fill in:
   - Title
   - Description
   - Category
   - Price (time credits or barter)
   - Photos (uploaded to IPFS)
   - Availability
4. Submit → Listed in marketplace
```

### Making a Trade

```
1. Find something you want
2. Tap "Message Seller"
3. Arrange details (time, location, etc.)
4. When complete:
   - Seller confirms delivery
   - Buyer confirms satisfaction
   - Credits transfer automatically
5. Rate the experience (builds trust)
```

### Barter (Direct Trade)

```
1. Go to Marketplace → "Barter"
2. Post what you have and what you want
3. Example: "I have 3 jars of homemade jam, want bicycle tune-up"
4. Others can match directly
5. No credits needed — direct exchange
```

## Governance

Your voice matters. Participate in decisions that affect your community.

### How Governance Works

```
Four levels:

Individual → Your privacy, data, skills
Community → Local projects, rules, resources
Network State → UBI, trade, infrastructure
Federation → Global issues, climate, rights

Decisions are made at the lowest level possible.
Your neighborhood handles its own garden; UBI policy is set statewide.
```

### Viewing Proposals

```
1. Go to Governance
2. See all active proposals:
   - 📋 Draft (community feedback)
   - 💬 Discussion (open for comments)
   - 🗳️ Voting (cast your vote!)
   - ✅ Passed / ❌ Failed
3. Tap any proposal to see full details
```

### Creating a Proposal

```
1. Go to Governance → "New Proposal"
2. Choose level:
   - Community (local changes)
   - Network State (regional policy)
3. Fill in:
   - Title
   - Description (AI can help you write it)
   - What you're asking for
   - Expected impact
   - Budget (if applicable)
4. Submit → 7-day discussion period → 7-day vote
```

### Voting

```
1. Go to Governance → "Voting"
2. See all proposals open for voting
3. Tap a proposal to see:
   - Full text
   - AI summary of pros/cons
   - Similar past proposals
   - Current vote tally
4. Vote: 👍 Yes, 👎 No, 🤷 Abstain
5. Your vote is encrypted — no one can see how you voted
```

### AI Governance Assistant

```
Ask the AI to help you understand proposals:

/circle ai summarize proposal 42
/circle ai what are the pros and cons of proposal 42
/circle ai how did similar proposals vote in the past
/circle ai what does this proposal mean for me personally
```

## Your Personal Node

Your Circlenomic app is more than an app — it's a personal node in the network.

### What Your Node Does

```
Your Personal Node:
├── Stores your identity (encrypted, local only)
├── Maintains your economic history
├── Runs your AI assistant (local LLM)
├── Connects to community DHT
├── Syncs with Matrix for communication
├── Participates in governance voting
├── Hosts your marketplace listings
└── Backs up your data (encrypted)
```

### Privacy Controls

```
1. Go to Settings → Privacy
2. Control what's shared:
   - Profile visibility (community, network, public)
   - Transaction history (private by default)
   - Location (optional)
   - Skills (visible to community)
   - Reputation (visible to community)
3. Use ZK proofs to verify without revealing:
   - Age (prove 18+ without showing birthday)
   - Membership (prove you're a member without showing which community)
   - Skills (prove certification without showing details)
```

### Backup & Recovery

```
1. Go to Settings → Backup
2. Your backup phrase is shown ONCE at creation
3. Write it down and store safely
4. If you lose your device:
   - Install Circlenomic on new device
   - Enter backup phrase
   - Your node restores from the network
5. If you lose your phrase:
   - Use social recovery (3 trusted contacts)
   - Or contact community support
```

### Node Performance

```
Your node runs efficiently:
• CPU: <5% average usage
• RAM: 200-500MB
• Storage: 1-5GB (grows with usage)
• Network: Minimal (only syncs when needed)
• Battery: <2% drain per day on mobile

Works offline:
• Queue transactions locally
• Sync when connection restored
• AI assistant works without internet
• Encrypted local storage
```

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What are time credits?

Time credits are the internal currency of Circlenomic. They represent hours of service and are used to:
- Pay for services from other members
- Access community resources
- Buy goods in the marketplace
- Earn through providing services

Unlike money, time credits:
- Are earned equally (1 hour = 1 credit)
- Have built-in circulation incentive (2% monthly demurrage)
- Cannot be hoarded (maximum 500 credits)
- Are not backed by any government

### How is this different from regular money?

```
Regular Money:
• Value based on market forces
• Can be hoarded
• Wealth concentrates
• Interest benefits lenders

Time Credits:
• Value = 1 hour of work (equal for everyone)
• Demurrage encourages circulation
• Distribution is more equal
• No interest, no debt
• Focused on community, not profit
```

### Is my data safe?

```
Yes. Your data is:

• Stored locally on YOUR device
• Encrypted with YOUR keys
• Never sent to external servers without permission
• Backed up encrypted (only you can decrypt)
• Private by default

The network sees:
• Your public identity (username, community)
• Your verified skills (with your permission)
• Your marketplace listings (if you choose to share)

The network does NOT see:
• Your transaction history
• Your balance
• Your private messages
• Your voting choices
• Your location
```

### What if I don't have internet?

```
Circlenomic works offline:

• Queue transactions locally
• Use AI assistant offline
• Read cached content
• Compose messages
• Draft proposals

When connection restores:
• Everything syncs automatically
• Conflicts resolved by timestamp
• No data lost

For extended offline use:
• Mesh networking available
• LoRaWAN support for rural areas
• Community hubs with WiFi
```

### How do I earn more time credits?

```
Ways to earn:

1. Provide services → 1 credit/hour
2. Teach others → 1.5 credits/hour
3. Mentor someone → 1.25 credits/hour
4. Participate in governance → 10 credits/vote
5. Complete education → 20 credits/course
6. Verify skills → 5 credits/skill used
7. Community service → 15 credits/hour
8. Organize events → 15 credits/hour
9. Create educational content → 10 credits/course
10. Help new members → 5 credits/new member
```

### What if I disagree with a governance decision?

```
You always have:

1. Voice
   • Propose changes
   • Vote on proposals
   • Attend town halls
   • Speak in Matrix rooms
   • Run for community leadership

2. Exit
   • Leave any community
   • Take your reputation with you

3. Appeal
   • Dispute resolution process

   • Cross-community arbitration
```
